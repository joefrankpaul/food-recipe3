FRIED CHICKEN

Fried chicken is a favorite family meal for many occassions. Make your own at home using a cut up fryer chicken (or a whole chicken brokon down into parts) to enjoy fried chicken at its most flavorful.this is a simple, traditional recipes to make fried chicken using either a stove top or a deep fryer. if you'dlike, please feel free to add more seasonings to your favorite taste. let us know your favorite mix for fried chicken in the coments below and maybe we'll add up your recipe to to the site. enjoy!

  • INGREDIENTS

    • there are different types of ingredient
      1. chicken
      2. egg
      3. cup flour
      4. tsp kosher salt
      5. tsp pepper
      6. tsp garlic powder
      7. tsp paprika
      8. vegetable oil

    DIRECTIONS

    1. in a large bowl, beat the eggs, in another bowl, combine the the salt, pepper, garlic powder and paprika to make a seasoned salt add the flour to a third

    2. prepare the chicken by first seasoning each piece of chicken to your taste with the seasoned salt. then dip each piece in the egg and drag through flour until coated well.

    3. fill a large pot or deep fryer half full with oil and heat to 350'f. pace the chicken parts in and fry until dark and crisp. the thighs and the legs will take longer to cook - 15min than the breast and wings, which will take about 10min

    4. remove from heat, let sit for 5min to cool and enjoy.
